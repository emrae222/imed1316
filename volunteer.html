Volunteer With The Run Better Project

Help support runners by assisting with events, workshops, training sessions, and community activities.

Why Volunteer?

Volunteers help make The Run Better Project welcoming, organized, and encouraging for everyone. Whether you enjoy running, helping others, or being part of a positive community, volunteering is a great way to get involved.

How You Can Help

Volunteers can help with check-ins, water stations, event setup, beginner runner support, and encouraging participants during workshops or runs.
