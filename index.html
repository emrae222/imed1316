The Run Better Project is designed to help runners improve their form, endurance, and overall confidence. Whether someone is just starting out or trying to become more consistent, this site provides simple tips for breathing, pacing, stretching, and staying motivated. Running is not only about speed — it is also about building healthy habits, listening to your body, and making progress one step at a time.
