The Run Better Project Schedule

Training Schedule

The Run Better Project offers a simple weekly training schedule for runners who want to improve endurance, speed, breathing, and recovery. Each day focuses on a different part of running so participants can build strength safely and stay consistent.

  1. Warm up before every run
  2. Focus on proper breathing and posture
  3. Take rest and recovery days seriously
  4. Track your progress each week

What to Bring

Participants should wear comfortable running shoes and athletic clothing. Bring water, a towel, and a positive attitude. The schedule is designed to help runners improve without feeling overwhelmed.

  • Running shoes
  • Water bottle
  • Comfortable workout clothes
  • Notebook or phone to track progress

Who Can Join?

This schedule is made for beginner and intermediate runners. Whether you are training for your first mile, a 5K, or just trying to build a healthy habit, The Run Better Project gives you a simple plan to follow throughout the week.
